Benefits to health care

Expert systems

Expert systems allow the user to interogate a vast knowledge of information, allowing users to get a diagonosis without going to a GP, saving GP's, and the user's time. Whilst some might argue that the cost of producing one of these to be reliable is high, the long-term benefits greatly outweigh the negatives and risks.

Booking systems

Booking systems have made it easier than ever for customers to book and track appointments likewise for GPS and hospitals to track and manage said appointments. Whilst this doesn't sound important or revolutionary, without simple algorithms, we would never have regular checkups automatiacally sent to us.

Technology development

As a result of computer science, technology in hospitals has increased dramatically! We now have nfusion pumps, dialysis machines, antibiotics, heart valves, MRI scanners, even hand washing stations, all of which were once just fantasy have resulted in millions of lives being saved.

Covid-19

Thanks to computer science, the government were able to develop an app, notifing people if they have been in close contact with someone who has tested positive for covid-19. This happened to be greatly beneficial as it helped people know when to isolate, and know if they had to do a test, saving resources and lives.
